Dark Project Job Guide

This is the how to for all the jobs in our server so far.

Job Discriptions

Gas Attendant

Head to the job center, you can find it on the map. Once you get to the job center open up the job menu and select the Gas attendant job. Once the job has been selected you'll be able to find the job blips on the map, look for the oil refiners' locker room to go on shift. Once you get to the locker room open up the menu and select the work clothes, after you've done that you'll be able to spawn yourself a work truck at the vehicle spawner located within view of the locker room marker. There is a $2000-dollar deposit for the truck that'll you get back minus any damages to the truck, so drive safe.
Once you've got your work truck, open up the map and find the drill for oil marker and head there. Once you get to the drill site get out of the truck and head into the yellow marker and press E to begin drilling for oil. Continue doing that until you can't hold any more oil, then head back to your truck and find the refine oil marker on the map and head there. Once you get to refine oil get out of the truck head to the marker to begin the process, once its completed get back in you truck and find the mix refined oil marker. Head there and mix the refined oil and then head back to your truck and find the deliver gas marker.
After you've completed this you may do it again or return the truck to the red marker and get your deposit back and your pay. The red marker for this job is by the delivery location. Also, don't forgot to change back into your civilian clothes.

Fashion

There is a $200-dollar deposit for the truck that'll you get back minus any damages to the truck, so drive safe.
Once you've got your work truck, open up the map and find the Wool marker and head there. Once you get to the Wool Marker get out of the truck and head into the yellow marker and press E to begin collecting wool. Continue doing that until you can't hold anymore wool, then head back to your truck and find the Dress Designer's Locker Room marker on the map and head there. Once you get back to the dress designers warehouse get out of the truck head to the marker make fabric to begin the process, once its completed find the retrieve clothing marker in the same room and grab the clothing. After that's completed you can head to the delivery point which is a clothing store marker on the map. Head there and go into the backroom to sell them clothing and then head back to your truck.
After you've completed this you may do it again or return the truck to the red marker and get your deposit back and your pay. The red marker for this job is in front of the warehouse. Also, don't forgot to change back into your civilian clothes.

Mechanic

Head to the job center, you can find it on the map. Once you get to the job center open up the job menu and select the Mechanic job. Once the job has been selected you'll be able to find the job blips on the map, look for the Mechanic's Shop to go on shift. Once you get to the shop open up the menu closest to the office and select the work clothes, after you've done that you'll be able to spawn yourself a work truck at the vehicle spawner located within view of the locker room marker.
This job has a work menu that can be reached by pressing f6. All the actions and functions needed are located in that menu. There's another menu for crafting repair kits and such but you'll need to have the right Mechanic position to access it. You need to /twt to the server that you are on duty and ready for a job.
Whenever you've had enough head back to the shop return your vehicles and work equipment. Also note that f7 is for billing customers. Be sure to change your clothes back.

Police

Head to the job center, you can find it on the map. Once you get to the job center open up the job menu and select the Police job. Once the job has been selected you'll be able to find the job blips on the map, look for the Main Blue Police Station to go on shift. Once you get to the police station head inside and find the locker rooms. There are little plaques on the walls with arrows pointing to the police station areas. Once in the locker room head to the yellow marker that allows you to select your clothing. Put on the police outfit and head over to the armory and gear up. After you've done that you'll be able to spawn yourself a police vehicle at the vehicle spawner located at the parking lot outside.
This job has a work menu that can be reached by pressing f6. All the actions and functions needed are located in that menu. There's another menu for crafting repair kits and such but you'll need to have the right position to access it.
Whenever you've had enough head back to the police return your vehicles and work equipment. Getting caught using police equipment outside of the shift time is forbidden and you could be banned. Also note that f7 is for billing customers. Be sure to change your clothes back.
